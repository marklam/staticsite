In the pipeline

We're developing a new kind of Mass Spectrometry software for bio-signature discovery.We want to take the DIA Tandem-MS data you run (or may already have) and search for the significant features within it:

  • Even if they're a change in differential expression
  • Even if they're modified forms
  • Even if they've not been commonly observed
  • And especially if they're not what you were expecting to find

Hypothesis-free

Performing identifications up-front, inserts a filter on what you can discover.

The targets are limited to the analytes you thought were possible, and then accepted as being present.

More

Multivariate

Not everything in biology is a 2-fold change. Some changes are relative, and some require context.

Only studying the big changes ignores information that could enhance the diagnostic performance.

More

Unbiased

A peptide spectral library could bias your results - towards changes in well-known proteins, and away from new discoveries.

We highlight the significant fragment differences, helping you ensure that special attention is paid to them during your identification efforts.

More

Hands-free

The more settings your software has, the harder it can be for anyone to reproduce your results.

We remove the opportunity to tweak settings in search of improved search results.
